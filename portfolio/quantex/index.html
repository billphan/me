QUANTEX, MANUFACTURES AND LAUNCHES
ADVANCED ROCKETS AND SPACECRAFTS.

Quantex Industries is a national aerospace company that focuses on the development and launching of advanced rocket systems and spacecrafts designed with the future in mind. Cooperating with notible space and engineering companies like NASA and Lockheed Martin, Quantex assists in manned and unmanned space missions to the International Space Station and beyond. Quantex Industries is also the leading manufacturer in portable aerospace technologies such as outfits, communications technology that assist astronauts on their journey to and from space. We here at Quantex faces the future, solving great problems of our time. Continuously innovating technologies that will define the future of manned and unmanned space travel.
