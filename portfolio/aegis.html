Aegis Secure Storage Mobile Task App

Aegis is an iOS mobile application concept that emphasizes digital storage and security protocols while focusing on a sleek modern design and user friendly interface.

Abstract

In today's digital age we are burdened with a large amount of passwords, usernames, sensitive data, and documents that can at times be difficult to manage and recall on the spot. Losing and forgetting passwords can lead to potentially major security problems if not managed especially when it comes to much more sensitive information such as digital banking and/or online shopping. The Aegis concept is an app that makes use of the touch ID fingerprint sensor, passcode, and 2-step verification to secure your information.
