Abstract

The Tesla Motors mobile app redesign is an attempt to restructure the interface of the mobile application. Exploring further input methods such as Apple's TouchID mechanic to unlock a Tesla as opposed to a tap or swipe feature.

Not being able to own a Tesla made me want to peek inside the app, but not being able to access the app has led me to reimagine an idea of what it could be like. This was an attempt to do so taking into consideration of the latest software features and security mechanism of the vehicle.
